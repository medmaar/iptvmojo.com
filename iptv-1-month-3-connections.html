No contracts, no automatic renewals. The 1-month plan is pay-as-you-go — renew only if you choose to continue.
How do I receive my IPTV credentials after ordering? +
After confirming your order, we send your M3U link or Xtream Codes (server URL, username, password) directly to your email or WhatsApp within minutes — day or night, including weekends.
What does “3 Connections” mean? +
3 connections means up to 3 devices can stream simultaneously — on separate TVs, phones, or tablets. Perfect for households with multiple viewers.
Which devices are compatible? +
IPTVmojo works on Amazon Firestick, Samsung & LG Smart TVs, Android TV boxes, Android phones & tablets, iPhone, iPad, Apple TV, MAG boxes, Windows PCs, and Macs. See our setup guide.
Are sports and PPV events included? +
Yes — all sports and PPV events are included at no extra charge. NFL, NBA, Champions League, UFC, Boxing, Formula 1, NHL — all in your $27 plan.
