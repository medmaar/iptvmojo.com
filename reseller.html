Reseller Questions

What exactly is included in each reseller plan? +
Every plan includes 1 full year of access for the stated number of simultaneous connections, a dedicated reseller panel, white-label support, and 24/7 backend support. All connections include the full IPTVmojo library: 50,000+ channels, 300,000+ VOD, and all sports & PPV.
Can I start with 10 connections and upgrade later? +
Yes. You can upgrade your plan at any time. Contact us via WhatsApp or email and we'll arrange the upgrade with the remaining time on your current plan taken into account.
Do I need technical knowledge to manage the panel? +
No technical background is required. The reseller panel is straightforward — creating accounts, setting expiry dates, and monitoring connections are all done with a few clicks. We walk you through it when you get access.
Can I use my own brand name with my clients? +
Yes — white-label is fully supported. Your clients interact entirely under your brand. IPTVmojo remains invisible in the background.
What payment methods are accepted for reseller plans? +
We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, Bitcoin, USDT, and bank transfer. Contact us via email or WhatsApp after submitting your order and we'll arrange payment.
