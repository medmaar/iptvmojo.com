Frequently Asked Questions

What is an IPTV service and how does IPTVmojo work? +
IPTV (Internet Protocol Television) delivers live TV channels and on-demand content over your internet connection instead of cable or satellite. IPTVmojo provides you with a login (M3U link or Xtream codes) that you enter into any compatible IPTV app to access 50,000+ channels and 300,000+ titles instantly.
What internet speed do I need for IPTV? +
A minimum of 15 Mbps is required for HD streaming. We recommend 30 Mbps for reliable HD and 50+ Mbps if you plan to stream in 4K or use multiple connections simultaneously.
