Frequently asked questions

Frequently asked questions

What's the picture and sound quality like?

Our service offers content in Full HD up to 4K, including SD, HD and 4K options.

What types of channels and content are available?

Access a vast selection of Quebec, French, sports and news channels, as well as the main channels from Canada, the U.S., France and other countries.

Can I customize my channel list?

Yes, depending on your device. For example, with Formuler boxes, add favorite channels via the colored buttons on the remote control.

Is my device compatible?

Our subscriptions are compatible with Firestick, Formuler, Apple TV, smartphones, Smart TVs, and more.

How does the system work?

With a Formuler box, installation is preconfigured. For all other devices, we send a connection guide after payment.

Are there any hidden fees or subscriptions?

No, there are no hidden costs. All our services are sold directly and transparently.

What is your refund and cancellation policy?

Formuler boxes include a one-year guarantee. Subscriptions are non-refundable.

What Internet speed do I need?

A 30 Mb/s connection is required, 60 Mb/s is recommended.

Is the service available internationally?

Yes, our service is available in several countries.

Do you offer customer support?

Yes, 24-hour assistance is available via our chatbot.

Can I terminate the service at any time?

You can stop at any time, but the subscription remains active for the duration you paid for, with no refund.

Can I use the service on several televisions?

Yes, but one connection is required per device at a time. For multiple TVs simultaneously, additional subscriptions are required.

Do you accept payment in Bitcoin or USDT?

Yes, these options are available at checkout.

Is there a discount if I buy several boxes?

Yes, contact us and receive two months free with the purchase of two boxes.

Is there a sponsorship program?

Yes, if you refer a friend with your referral code, you get two months free on your subscription (on a 12-month subscription purchase).
