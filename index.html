Frequently asked questions

What is IPTVmojo and what does it offer?

IPTVmojo is a premium IPTV service giving you instant access to 50,000+ live TV channels, 300,000+ movies & series on demand, and premium PPV sports — all in HD and 4K quality. Plans start at just $9/month with no contracts required.

What picture and sound quality do you offer?

We stream in SD, HD, Full HD and up to 4K Ultra HD. Most premium sports channels and major live events broadcast in Full HD or 4K where available, delivering cinema-quality entertainment at home.

What channels and content are available?

You get 50,000+ live channels spanning North American, European, Middle Eastern, and international networks, plus 300,000+ movies and TV series on demand — including 2026 releases. Every plan also includes premium sports packages (NFL, NBA, NHL, UFC, PPV, F1), EPG guide, and Catch-up TV.

Are sports and PPV events included in all plans?

Yes. Every plan includes NFL, NBA, NHL, UFC, Boxing, Formula 1, Premier League, Champions League, La Liga, and more. PPV and major special events are included with all subscriptions at no extra charge — no add-ons required.

What devices are compatible with IPTVmojo?

IPTVmojo works on virtually any device: Amazon Firestick & Fire TV, Samsung and LG Smart TVs, Android TV boxes, Apple TV, iPhone, iPad, Android phones, Windows PC, Mac, MAG boxes, NVIDIA Shield, and more. Contact us via WhatsApp before ordering if you are unsure about device compatibility.

How does setup and installation work?

After confirming your order you receive your login credentials by email within minutes, plus a step-by-step configuration guide tailored to your device. Our WhatsApp support team is available 24/7 to walk you through setup — most customers are up and running in under 10 minutes.

How fast is activation after ordering?

Activation is typically instant — most users receive their credentials within minutes of payment confirmation. Our team monitors orders around the clock so even late-night orders are processed quickly. If there is any delay, our support team will reach out proactively.

Are there hidden fees or long-term contracts?

No hidden fees, ever. You pay the listed price and nothing else. There are no long-term contracts — pick the plan that suits you (1 month, 3 months, 6 months or 1 year) and renew only when you are ready. You are never locked in.

What Internet speed do I need?

For HD streaming a stable 10 Mb/s connection is sufficient. For 4K content we recommend at least 30 Mb/s. A wired Ethernet connection or strong Wi-Fi signal will give you the smoothest experience, especially during peak-time live sports.

Can I watch on multiple devices at the same time?

Each subscription includes 1 simultaneous connection by default. If you need to stream on multiple TVs at the same time simply select 2 to 10 connections using our device selector when ordering. Each additional connection is priced individually so you only pay for what you need.

Is IPTVmojo available worldwide?

Yes. IPTVmojo works in over 100 countries. As long as you have a stable internet connection you can stream your content anywhere — perfect for expats, travellers, and international households who want channels from their home country.

What customer support do you provide?

Our support team is available 24/7 via WhatsApp — real people, not bots. Whether you need help with setup, have a billing question, or experience a technical issue, we respond fast and actually resolve your problem. Customers consistently praise our support as one of the best in the IPTV industry.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit and debit cards, PayPal, and cryptocurrency payments including Bitcoin and USDT. All transactions are secure and encrypted. Contact us via WhatsApp if you prefer an alternative payment method or need assistance completing a payment.

We encourage all new customers to use our free 24-hour trial before subscribing so you can test the service risk-free. Subscriptions can be cancelled at any time but remain active until the end of the paid period. If you experience an unresolved technical issue, contact us and your case will be reviewed fairly.

Yes! We offer a free 24-hour trial so you can experience the full service before committing to a paid plan. Visit our free trial page and contact us via WhatsApp — no credit card required. Thousands of customers started with a free trial and never looked back.

The 1-Year plan includes a free IBO Player subscription — a premium IPTV player app with a clean interface, built-in EPG guide, multi-screen support and smooth playback. This is included at no extra cost and is one of the most popular IPTV players available.
