FAQ

IPTV Subscription Questions

How many connections do I need for my IPTV subscription? +
One connection allows one device to stream at a time. If you have multiple TVs or family members who want to watch simultaneously, you need multiple connections. We offer 1, 2, 3, 5, and 10-connection plans to suit households of any size.
What happens after my IPTV subscription expires? +
Your access simply stops when the subscription period ends — there are no automatic renewals or surprise charges. You can renew at any time by purchasing a new plan, and your credentials will be reactivated immediately.
Are subscriptions refundable? +
IPTV subscriptions are non-refundable once credentials have been delivered. This is why we strongly recommend using our free 24-hour trial to test the service before subscribing.
Can I upgrade my plan after purchasing? +
Yes — contact our 24/7 WhatsApp support and we'll help you upgrade your plan. The remaining time on your current plan is taken into account when calculating the upgrade cost.
Do I need to buy hardware to use IPTVmojo? +
No special hardware is required. IPTVmojo works on devices you already own: smart TVs, smartphones, tablets, Amazon Firestick, Apple TV, PC, and Mac. If you prefer a dedicated streaming box, we recommend Amazon Firestick or an Android TV box — but it's completely optional. See our full setup guide.
