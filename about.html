Our Story

Why We Built IPTVmojo

We started IPTVmojo because we believed TV should be simple, affordable, and available everywhere. Traditional cable is expensive, rigid, and tied to geography. Streaming services fragment your content across 5+ subscriptions. We built the alternative: a single subscription that gives you everything.

Today, IPTVmojo serves customers in over 100 countries — from Canadians who want local channels while abroad, to international households who want content from their home country. Every plan is the same flat price. Every plan includes the same full channel list. No tiers, no upsells, no surprises.
