What's the fastest way to get support? +
WhatsApp is always fastest. We monitor WhatsApp 24/7 and typically respond within a few minutes. Email responses may take a few hours depending on volume.
I didn't receive my credentials after payment — what do I do? +
First, check your spam/junk folder. If you still can't find the email after 10 minutes, contact us on WhatsApp with your payment confirmation and we'll activate your account manually within minutes.
My IPTV channels are buffering or not loading — how do I fix it? +
Buffering is usually caused by your internet connection. Try: (1) restart your router, (2) connect via Ethernet instead of WiFi, (3) close other apps using bandwidth. If the issue persists, contact us on WhatsApp — we'll check your connection from our end.
Can I change the email address my credentials are sent to? +
Yes — contact us on WhatsApp and we can resend your credentials to a different email address.
