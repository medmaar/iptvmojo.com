FAQ

Setup & Technical Questions

What is a M3U playlist and do I need it? +
An M3U playlist is a file containing links to all your IPTV channels. Most apps let you add channels by pasting the M3U URL directly. Alternatively, Xtream Codes login (server + username + password) is an even easier method supported by all major IPTV apps. We provide both formats.
How long does activation take? +
Usually under 5 minutes. Once payment is confirmed, our system automatically sends your credentials to your email. If you don't see the email within 10 minutes, check your spam folder or contact our WhatsApp support.
Can I install IPTVmojo on multiple devices? +
Yes — you can install the app and enter your credentials on as many devices as you like. However, only the number of connections in your plan can stream simultaneously. For example, a 1-connection plan means only one device can watch at a time, even if the app is installed on multiple devices.
What if I need help with setup? +
Contact our 24/7 WhatsApp support team. We help customers with Firestick, Smart TV, Android, Apple TV, and PC setup every day. Real human support, fast response, every day of the year — no chatbots.
