Device Setup Guide

Follow the step-by-step instructions for your device below. Click any device to expand its guide.

Step 1: Download Smarters Player Lite from the App Store:
apps.apple.com — Smarters Player Lite

  1. Open the app and tap Login With Xtream Codes API.
  2. Enter the Username, Password and Portal URL we send you after subscription.
  3. Tap ADD USER and wait a few seconds for your playlist to load.
  4. Tap the Live TV icon to browse channels.
  5. Select a channel group, then tap a channel name. Double-tap the small player to go full screen.
  6. No EPG URL is needed — just tap Install EPG when prompted.

Step 1: Download the Smarters APK directly:
iptvsmarters.com/smarters.apk

  1. Open the app and tap Login With Xtream Codes API.
  2. Enter your Username, Password, and Portal URL, then tap ADD USER.
  3. Wait a few seconds for the playlist to load.
  4. Tap Live TV → select your channel group → tap a channel to watch.
  5. Tap the small player and expand to full screen to enjoy.
