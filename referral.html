Do I need to be a current subscriber to refer? +
Yes — you need an active IPTVmojo subscription to participate in the referral program. The 2 free months are added to your existing account.
When do the free months get added? +
Free months are added to both accounts within 24 hours of your friend's 12-month payment being confirmed.
Can I refer more than one person? +
Absolutely — there is no limit. Every successful referral earns you 2 more free months. If you refer 10 friends who each buy a 12-month plan, you get 20 months free.
What if my friend buys a shorter plan? +
The referral reward only applies when your friend purchases a 12-month subscription. Shorter plans (1 month, 3 months, 6 months) do not qualify for the referral bonus.
How do I check that my referral was credited? +
Contact our WhatsApp support team and they can confirm your referral status and account expiry date at any time.
